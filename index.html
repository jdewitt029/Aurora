Aurora Aura (2020)

Photoshop
1000x1000 px

This design is a cover art for a song that I made for a DJ named SKNIBLK. Environment building is something that I have always been a fan of and this was one of my first attempts. I tried to create a mood that is almost out of this world in this piece. I created this using many different skills that I have developed in Photoshop. While keeping in mind things like- color harmony, composition, value, and many more- I created an environment with the DJ as the subject standing in an other worldly place.
